As a web developer and freelance WordPress developer, I enjoy creating projects for non-profits and small businesses to get their feet out of the door and into the big world so they will be seen by everyone. I work on cutting edge web programming, and I am constantly learning and improving my crafts. WordPress is my go-to CMS tool. Whether it's picking the right theme for you or customizing a brand new one, I will bring your dreams to reality.

A little eccentric but you will come to know that I am dependable, and laid back. I crave the unknown - each client is like another attempt to see a new world from a different mind. I will piece your wants and needs to fruition. This is something I enjoy doing. I won't waste your time because my play is my work and I get it done!
